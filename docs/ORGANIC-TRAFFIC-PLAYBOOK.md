## Facebook Group Posting Rules

Use soft, helpful posts. Do not spam groups with the same sales message.

Good format:

> We are testing compressed foam sofas for Spain-first delivery. The main use case is apartments where a normal sofa is hard to move through stairs or lifts. I wrote a quick checklist for measuring the route before ordering: [guide link]. Feedback from renters or people in old buildings is very welcome.

Avoid:

- Repeating the same post in many groups on the same day.
- Posting only a product link without helpful context.
- Promising faster delivery than the published 23-30 working day estimate.
- Offering discounts, refunds or policy exceptions in comments.
